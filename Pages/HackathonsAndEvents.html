Hackathons and Events

Robotica - 2024:

Soon after I was appointed as the Vice-President of our institute's Robotics club, took the initiate of organising an yearly event which goes and becomes a tradition within the college. It was the time for the club to raise it's standard in terms of happening. It was a two-day event on 1st and 2nd of March, 2024. It included four events:

  1. 1. Ideathon
  2. 2. Skydash
  3. 3. Goal rush
  4. 4. Full throttle

It was first time such an event was conducted. The prepartion started well before 40 days from the date of the event. Many aspects like marketing, sponsorships, logistics, finances, arena prepartion, jury were taken care by our team of 25 people.

First day:

The first day was all about ideathon. Participants were given 17 Sustainable Development Goals out of which one topic is to be chosen and work on the same. Well over 100 people showed up not just from within the college but also from various institutions within the state. It began with a resource person explaining the importance of SDG goals and why it is important. Certain parameter where chosen upon which the judgement criteria was designed.

Second day:

Second day of the event was the combination of ideathon presentations as well as the other three technical events happening simulataneously. Many students from other students also showed up and expressed their interest in our event. The total cash prize of 80,000 Rupees attracted a huge mass and made it a grand success. Students of freshman year felt inspired and learnt new lessons from the same. With well over 250 participants, it did bring a lot of enthusiasm.

3D Printing Workshop - 2023:

As part of the Robotics club of our institution, we have organised a workshop to enhance our members understanding in 3D printing on 3rd August, 2023. Many curious students signed up and expressed their interest in the field of electronics. It was a one-day event which included the CAD software followed by live demo of working of a 3D printer. It was a fun and enriching experience for the resource persons, organisers and students alike.These events provide much insightful time rather than regular theory sessions. It was a great success thanks to the team of Robotics Club of Geethanjali College of Engineering and Technology.

Elecsta Ideathon - 2023:

Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC) organised an event 'Elecsta Ideathon' on 23rd and 24th of July, 2024. The task was to come up with an idea to develop a technology or tool in one of the many available domains. The time duration given to come up with the respective idea was around 24 hours. The topic given to my team is disaster management. Me along with my team brain stormed many ideas but later found that most of them are already in use and does barely require any improvement. So, an idea of developing an ML model to study weather patterns and draw necessary insights was proposed. Even though it is hard to implement, the idea of making use of AI & ML was appreciated. Improvements over the existing system were highlighted which resulted in us securing 2nd prize in the event. It was a great start in our hackthon journey and granted us enormous confidence.
